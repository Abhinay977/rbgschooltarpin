OUR HISTORY

Government Secondary School Tarpin is 58 kilometers away from the capital Gangtok, nestled in a rural area in the Pakyong district of Sikkim under the 19 Rhenock constituency.

The school was established in the year 1960 and is situated in the heart of 08 Tarpin Gram Panchayat Unit.

SCHOOL VISION & MISSION

Vision: To inspire students to be social, adaptable, resourceful, caring, visionary, and better human beings.

Mission: To provide the best opportunity for every student to enhance their potential, knowledge, and caliber.

The school nurtures responsibility and empathy among students for society.
